-----------------------
Installation Procedure 
-----------------------

 1. Plug the adapter into one of the Master PCI slots of your PC.

 2. Power on your PC.

 3. Open Windows 98 operating system.

 4. Windows 98 will prompt "PCI Ethernet Controller" in "Add New Hardware
    Wizard" dialog box, then click <NEXT> button.

 5. Select "Search for the best driver for your device" from "Add New Hardware 
    Wizard" dialog box, then click <NEXT> button.

 6. Insert driver disk bundled with "Myson MTD80X based 100/10 PCI Ethernet
    Adapter".

 7. Enter the driver pathname, then click the <NEXT> button from "Add New 
    Hardware Wizard" dialog box.

    Example :

             A:\WIN98
      
 8. Select your installed adapter - "Myson MTD80X based 100/10 PCI Ethernet 
    Adapter", then click the <NEXT> button from the dialog box.

 9. To complete the process, please follow the instructions on the screen.

10. Restart your computer.
